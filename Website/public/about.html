About Us

Xvirus is a cybersecurity project founded in 2010 with a clear mission: to provide consumers with simple yet powerful tools to keep their computers protected, clean, and running at peak performance. What began as a small initiative has grown into a trusted name in the security industry, recognized by leading platforms worldwide.

Our flagship product, Xvirus Anti-Malware, combines real-time protection, advanced malware scanning, and ransomware defense into a lightweight package that works seamlessly alongside your existing antivirus. We also offer Xvirus Firewall, giving users full control over their network connections, and the Xvirus SDK/CLI, a scanning engine trusted by industry partners such as OPSWAT, PolySwarm, VirScan, and HerdProtect.

Over the years, we have earned multiple awards and certifications — including the OPSWAT Gold Certification and top ratings from platforms like MajorGeeks, Softpedia, and Software Informer. With millions of downloads and a growing user base, we remain committed to continuous improvement and innovation.

We believe everyone deserves access to reliable cybersecurity. That's why our core products remain free for personal use, with Pro upgrades available for those who want the full experience. Help us grow by suggesting improvements or recommending Xvirus to your friends and family — so they too can browse the internet safely and enjoy their computers at top performance.
